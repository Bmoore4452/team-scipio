DJ SCIPIO

Anita is Bronx, America boy turned professional woman DJ-SCIPIO

Anita C. Scipio is a Bronx, America boy turned professional woman. A Jersey graduate from Montclair High School. Anita attended Fairleigh Dickinson University/FIT where she majored in Marketing/Communications and Business. In college she was in the Gospel Choir, Ski club and also involved in other clubs and community events. While studying for her degree business she started to also pursue her passion for music and entertaining as a professional DJ in New Jersey. Her DJ career has spanned over three decades.

Among her debut, she was nominated by the first ever Juice Awards for Hip Hop Female DJ. Afterwards she was also nominated for best new female DJ. SCIPIO, has met and worked with many notable and legendary hip hop artists, singers, songwriters and producers. Additionally, she has worked alongside DJ Kid Capri, DJ Red Alert, etc. Unfortunately, Anita's skills began to deteriorate because of an injury due to falling during one of her sets. Additionally, due to a health related issue she has decided to pursue a career teaching young adults about DJ culture. SCIPIO has had the opportunity to work with some of the biggest names in hip-hop and R&B history. This inspired her to create many lasting memories for hip hop lovers and aspiring artists. She has performed at various events and venues ranging from private parties to entertainment showcases, corporate events, and many times at the infamous Amateur Night at The Apollo. From her life-long dedication to the world of music, Anita decided to create an interactive hip-hop journal for those who lived and loved this music evolution. An experience to celebrate her achievements and create a place for others to document a time capsule way for everyday music lovers, casual fans, and pro-rappers/aspiring artists to document music and entertainment.
